Recall Time ("the app", "we", "us") is a countdown and reminder app for iPhone,
developed by [DEVELOPER NAME]. We designed Recall Time to keep your data private:
the app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party
tracking. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

## Summary

- We do **not** collect, sell, or share your personal data.
- You don't create an account or log in.
- The countdowns you create are stored **on your device** and, if you're signed in
  to iCloud, in **your own private iCloud account** — not on any server we control.
- Notifications are scheduled **locally** on your device.
- The app contains **no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs**.

## Information you create

When you use Recall Time you can create countdowns that may include a title,
due date, notes, tags, a color, reminder times, and a category (such as birthday,
travel, or work). This information is:

- **Stored locally** on your device, and
- **Synced to your private iCloud database** (via Apple's CloudKit) when you are
  signed in to iCloud and iCloud Drive is enabled for Recall Time.

This data is used solely to display your countdowns and to schedule the reminders
you ask for. We have no access to it. It is not transmitted to us or to any
third party.

## iCloud and CloudKit

Recall Time uses Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) to back up your countdowns and sync them
across your devices. Your data is stored in your **private** iCloud database, which
is accessible only to you through your Apple ID. We cannot read or retrieve it.
Apple's handling of iCloud data is governed by
[Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

If you are not signed in to iCloud, the app still works and stores your data locally
on your device only.

## Notifications

If you allow notifications, Recall Time schedules **local** notifications on your
device to remind you about your countdowns. These are generated on-device and are
not sent through, or processed by, any server we operate. You can disable
notifications at any time in the iOS Settings app.

## What we do not do

- We do not collect or store any personal information on our own servers.
- We do not use analytics or crash-reporting services.
- We do not show advertising.
- We do not include third-party SDKs or trackers.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data stays under your control:

- Delete an individual countdown inside the app to remove it.
- Deleting the app removes its locally stored data from that device.
- To remove synced data from iCloud, sign in to iCloud and delete the app's data
  in **Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage**, or delete the
  countdowns within the app while signed in.

## Children's privacy

Recall Time does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including
children under the age of 13. The app does not require any personal information to
function.

## Security

Because your data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud account,
its security is protected by your device passcode/biometrics and your Apple ID
security. We do not maintain copies of your data.
